Dr. Tancreti lecturers in the areas of computer systems and software engineering in the Department of Computer Science at Iowa State University. He completed his undergraduate studies at the University of Iowa with bachelor degrees in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering and the PhD at Purdue University in Computer Engineering. After finishing his PhD, he co-founded a startup that develops products for the Internet of Things (IoT). He has worked on projects funded by the National Science Foundation, Air Force, Missile Defense Agency and international corporations.
